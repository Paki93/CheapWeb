Frequently Asked Questions

What do you need from me to start?

We need your logo (if available), main text (headline, short about paragraph), contact details, and 3–6 images (product, storefront, or team photos). If you don't have all assets, we can supply royalty-free images for a better look.

Can I edit the content later?

Yes — we provide simple instructions for updating text and images. If you prefer, we also offer affordable ongoing maintenance plans for updates and small changes.

Is hosting included?

Hosting is not automatically included — but we will advise a suitable, low-cost hosting provider and help configure your site. If you want, we can arrange hosting for you at an additional fee.

What if I need more than one page later?

You can upgrade to a multi-page package (Advanced / High Level). We can add pages later and provide a quote for the extra work required.
